## Why this matters

Where a person lives, and the support around them, shapes their daily life and
independence. Thinking about this early gives more time to explore what is
realistic and what they would prefer.

## What to think about

- Is the current home suitable for the longer term?
- Would the person prefer to live independently, with others, or in a
  supported setting?
- What level of day-to-day support would they need, and who could provide it?
- What is nearby that matters to them, such as transport, community spaces, or
  familiar people?

## Involving the person being cared for

Housing is deeply personal. Where you can, talk through the options with the
person and pay attention to what helps them feel settled and safe.
