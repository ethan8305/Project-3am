## Why this matters

A full life is more than care arrangements. Meaningful engagement, the work,
learning, hobbies and connections a person enjoys, is a big part of wellbeing.
Planning can help these continue, whatever changes come.

## What to think about

- What does the person enjoy doing, and what are they good at?
- Are there interests they would like to develop further?
- What kinds of activity suit them: paid work, volunteering, learning, or
  social and leisure time?
- Who or what helps them take part?

## Involving the person being cared for

Follow the person's own interests and pace. Engagement means most when it
reflects what they care about, not only what is convenient to arrange.
