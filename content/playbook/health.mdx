## Why this matters

If a caregiver is suddenly unavailable, it helps enormously for someone else to
be able to step in and understand the person's health quickly. Gathering this
information calmly, ahead of time, can make a stressful moment much easier.

## What to think about

- Who would look after the person temporarily in a medical emergency?
- Who is on their health support team, for example a doctor or counsellor?
- Is there a clear record of their health history and any regular needs?
- Are you familiar with the relevant health schemes and subsidies?

## Involving the person being cared for

Many people can take part in looking after their own health, with the right
support and clear information. Where you can, explain things simply and include
them in decisions about their care.
